Animal Examples

Lion

The lion is known as the king of the jungle. It is a powerful predator found in Africa.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals, known for their intelligence and social structures.

Penguin

Penguins are flightless birds that are highly adapted to cold environments.

Giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest mammals on Earth, known for their long necks and legs.

Panda

Pandas are native to China and are famous for their black-and-white fur and love of bamboo.
